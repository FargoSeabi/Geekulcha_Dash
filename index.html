Geekulcha UL is a student society at the University of Limpopo that aims to empower students with technology skills and foster innovation. We provide a platform for students to learn,collaborate, and create projects in various tech fields,including software development, data science and cybersecurity.Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and equip our members with the knowledge and resources needed to thrive in the tech industry.
